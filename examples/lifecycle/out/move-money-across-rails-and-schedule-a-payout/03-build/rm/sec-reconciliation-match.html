When to use

Treasury, ops, or finance surfaces reconciling money movement to the ledger.

When not to / use instead

Real-time pre-trade checks — this is post-settlement reconciliation, not an execution gate.

Behaviour & accessibility contract

Match state carries a text label plus icon, never color alone; manual-match is keyboard-operable and reversible with an audit note.

Regulatory anchor
