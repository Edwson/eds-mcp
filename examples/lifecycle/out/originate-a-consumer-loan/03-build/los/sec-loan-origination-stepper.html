Originate a consumer loan through disclosure and affordability gates before any commitment.

When to use

Any flow that takes a borrower from quote to signed loan agreement.

When not to / use instead

Servicing an existing loan — use RepaymentSchedule; collections — use CollectionsNotice.

Behaviour & accessibility contract

One decision per step; APR and total cost shown in words and figures before SIGN; SIGN is the only irreversible control and is gated behind explicit consent.

Regulatory anchor

TILA / Reg Z 12 CFR 1026 (cost-of-credit disclosure) · ECOA / Reg B 12 CFR 1002 (fair lending, no prohibited-basis fields) · UK FCA CONC 4 (pre-contract disclosure)
